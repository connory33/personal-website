Miata Restoration

I'm doing a full restoration of a 1995 Miata M-Edition. This is a fun project that my father and I began last summer due to our mutual love of cars.





This is a 1995 M-Edition. It was the last year of the NA engine in the Miata, and the only year they made this color, called "Merlot", so we got really lucky to find one in decent condition. It was owned by a mechanic previously, who managed to do a good job taking care of the engine, installing new fuel lines shortly before we bought it. Given the decent state of the engine, we opted to tackle the suspension first, as it appeared to be the most worn out system. However, we plan to eventually do a full restoration/replacement of the brakes, exhaust, wheels, interior, and exterior. Check back for updates and new pictures. Who knows, maybe we'll even get to turbocharging it eventually!
