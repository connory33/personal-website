About Me

My name is Connor Young and I'm from Palo Alto, CA. I have a BS in Mechanical Engineering and a minor in Business from Cornell University and a Master's in Operations Research and Information Engineering from Cornell Tech. I'm a logical and quantitative-minded person that enjoys using data and engineering to solve complex problems and inform decision-making. I aim to pursue data science as a career, and am especially interested in opportunities that combine engineering and analytics with my passion for the sports industry.
