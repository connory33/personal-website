Miata Restoration

My father and I recently purchased and began a restoration of a 1995 M-Edition Miata.





This is an awesome NA Miata with a '95 M-Edition-only paint color that we found on Craiglist and jumped on. The previous owner kept the engine in pretty good shape, so we're tackling the suspension first, but hopefully we'll eventually get to everything else! I'm excited to post updates here as we make progress.
